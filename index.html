Complete driving course

Class D — Full Licence Track

Covers theory classroom sessions, all practical hours on the circuit, attendance tracking, and JPJ exam scheduling — from your first lesson to your licence.

RM 1,200/ full course
Includes 9 supervised practical hours on the circuit plus theory classroom access. Payment is confirmed manually by our admin team after your registration.
  • Theory classroom sessions with the official curriculum module
  • 9 hours of supervised practical training on our circuit
  • Full access to slalom, hill start, sharp-turn and JPJ exam zones
  • Personal student portal — book slots, track hours, see results
  • One assigned instructor for the entire course
  • Official JPJ exam scheduling once you're ready
If you don't pass the JPJ exam: your first extra practice hour is free. Any additional hour after that is billed at RM 75/hour.
